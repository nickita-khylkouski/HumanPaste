The Engine

Six layers of
human behavior

We studied how real people actually type. The speed, the mistakes, the pauses when they think. Then we built software that does exactly that.

Where machines learn to hesitate.

01
Realistic key speed
Some keys are closer together than others. We model how fast your fingers actually move between each pair of keys.
02
Finger personality
Your index finger is fast. Your pinky is slow. We model each finger's timing individually, because that's how real typing works.
Your pinky is slower than your index. We know.
03
Natural typos
Wrong key, swapped letters, missed characters. The same kinds of mistakes you actually make, then caught and fixed, just like you would.
04
Thinking pauses
People slow down at the end of sentences, pause after commas, and hesitate before big words. So does HumanPaste.
05
False starts
Start typing one thing, change your mind, delete it, start over. That moment of indecision that proves you're human.
06
Fatigue & bursts
Speed drifts over time. Fast bursts, slow patches, gradual fatigue, second winds. Just like a real session.
